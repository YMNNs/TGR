关于TEDx高新路 About TEDxGaoxinRoad

TEDxGaoxinRoad由一群充满激情的志愿者组成，他们汇聚一堂，共同推动TED宣传Ideas worth spreading的理念。如今，TEDxGaoxinRoad继续完全自主运行，并得到许多公司和组织的支持。

The TEDxGaoxinRoad core organizing committee is made up of a group of passionate volunteers from a variety of disciplines who have come together to further the TED mission of giving voice to ideas worth spreading in our community. Now in its 3rd year, TEDxGaoxinRoad continues to be completely volunteer run and supported by many generous community-minded companies and organizations.

概述 At a Glance

TEDx高新路已于2017 年4 月正式得到官方授权，开始它在西安的各项活动。两年来，TEDx高新路保持初心，传承TED “IdeasWorth Spreading” 精神，通过沙龙、大会等活动，用年轻人的想象力和行动力凝聚古都西安各处的优秀人士，力求给西安大众一次精神的洗礼，传播优秀思想。

The TEDxGaoxinRoad is mainly intended for citizens who live, work or study at Xi’an. The purpose is to pass on the essence of “ideas worth spreading”. we gather the outstanding talents from our ancient city, promote innovative ideas, give the audience a spiritual baptism, spread the splendid thoughts and share the sound experiences.

组织 Organization

TEDx高新路是面对全西安市市民的一次大型公益演讲活动。团队均由不超过20 岁的青年组成，是向西安、向全国展示西安青年形象的一次大型活动。

TEDxGaoxinRoad is a large-scale public speech in the face of the citizens of Xi’an. The team consists of young people up to 20 years old. It is a large-scale event to show Xi’an youth images to Xi’an.

宗旨 Goal

用青年人的视角，去审视西安的发展、未来、科技、教育、传奇；用青年人的想象力、行动力凝聚古都西安各处的优秀人士，让青年人的优秀思想给西安大众一次精神的洗礼。

As a new stream of blood in the TEDx family, our TGR team will present the youngest and the fullest vigour to build a platform where diverse ideas are able to be spread.

TED及TEDx About TED and TEDx

TED简介TED是Technology, Entertainment, Design（科技，娱乐，设计)的英文缩写，是美国一家私有非盈利机构。该机构以它组织的TED大会著称，以“Ideas worth spreading”（传递优秀思想）为口号，其宗旨是“用思想的力量改变世界”。

在“传递优秀思想”这一价值的指引下，TED推出了一个叫TEDx的项目，所谓TEDx，就是指那些由本地TED粉丝自愿发起、自行组织的小型聚会，让本地的TED粉丝能够聚到一起，共享TED一刻。TED不会参与TEDx活动的组织，但是会对活动组织者给予指导和意见。

TED is a nonpartisan nonprofit devoted to spreading ideas, usually in the form of short, powerful talks. TED began in 1984 as a conference where Technology, Entertainment and Design converged, and today covers almost all topics — from science to business to global issues — in more than 110 languages. Meanwhile, independently run TEDx events help share ideas in communities around the world.

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxGaoxinRoad, where x = independently organized TED event. At our TEDxGaoxinRoad event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.
